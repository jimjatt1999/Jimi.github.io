My Gymnastic Rings Journey

My Love for Gymnastic Rings

I fell in love with gymnastic rings during my studies in Tokyo from 2019 to 2020. As the pandemic unfolded, these versatile tools became my go-to for staying fit and challenging myself when the gym closed down. Since then, they've been an integral part of my fitness routine.

My Favorite Exercises

  • Bulgarian Ring Dips
  • Ring Muscle Ups
  • Ring Semi Handstands
  • Ring Pull-ups

These exercises not only build strength but also improve balance, flexibility, and body control. The constant instability of the rings makes every workout a full-body challenge.

My Progress

From struggling with basic support holds in Tokyo to now performing complex movements, my journey with gymnastic rings has been transformative. They've not only shaped my physique but also instilled a sense of discipline and perseverance.
